Problem

In China, a large number of parents have struggled to balance their work and personal lives, and engage better with their kids. However, kids need timely guidance and feedback during the learning process, which can help them cultivate good learning habits. Especially for elementary school students, who are in a critical period to develop their self learning skills.

Solution

We created Dali Smart Lamp, an innovative learning tool with the combination of hardware and intelligent solutions, for elementary school students to help parents involved in children’s learning process and cultivate children’s self learning skills.

What is Dali Smart Lamp?

The product itself is a T-shaped desk lamp featuring a phone-like display, camera sensors and built-in digital assistant. All-in-one device focused on learning.

It is equipped with leading smart eye-protection technology that is specially designed for elementary school students. While ensuring eye health, it has multiple functions that can help children develop self learning skills and improve learning efficiency. The lamp can be linked with the app "Dali Parents" to help parents both have a life and be responsible about their children’s education.

How can Dali Smart Lamp be involved in the learning process?

Fingertips

supports Chinese and English bilingualism with high recognition accuracy

For English words, it can provide the pronunciation and meaning of the words, intelligently score based on the students’ pronunciation, and provide timely feedback to help the practice.

For Chinese words, a standard stroke order demonstration can be carried out to lay a good foundation for the words.

Besides, it supports content dictation real-time feedback according to the learning requirements of different school ages.

Online Study Room

Study with Buddies

We hold online Study with Buddies rooms for different subjects every night, where students can not only study with friends of the same age, but also get online tutoring from real teachers. In addition, teachers will let students who perform well become role models for other students, which can effectively empower students' learning motivation.

Self Study

Students can make their own learning plans, guide learning through functions such as word-searching and voice questioning at the fingertips of the desk lamp, and upload their finished homework with one click to parents who can check it. After completing the plan, students will receive system rewards or unlock "medals", and the rewards can be used to watch some interesting stories or popular science.

Dual Camera Video Call

enables parents to tutor their kids and check in remotely via a mobile app

When parents work overtime or travel on business and cannot guide their children in real time, Dali Smart Lamp allows parents to communicate with their children at any time. During the call, parents can freely switch between the front camera and the top camera, checking in with children proactively, understanding their learning difficulties, and providing guidance if needed.
