Problem

In China, numerous parents face challenges in juggling their work and personal responsibilities while actively engaging with their children. Meanwhile, children require timely guidance and feedback throughout their learning journey to foster effective study habits, especially during the crucial stage of elementary school when they develop essential self-learning skills.

Solution

We created Dali Smart Lamp, a cutting-edge learning tool specifically designed for elementary school students. By seamlessly integrating hardware and intelligent solutions, Dali Smart Lamp empowers parents to actively engage in their children's learning process while nurturing the development of essential self-learning skills.

What is Dali Smart Lamp?

Dali Smart Lamp is an innovative, all-in-one learning device designed as a T-shaped desk lamp.

It features a phone-like display, camera sensors, and a built-in digital assistant, making it a comprehensive tool tailored for elementary school students. With advanced smart eye-protection technology, the lamp prioritizes eye health while offering a range of functions to enhance children's self-learning skills and improve overall learning efficiency. Moreover, the lamp can be seamlessly connected with the "Dali Parents" app, enabling parents to strike a balance between their personal lives and active involvement in their children's education.

How can Dali Smart Lamp be involved in the learning process?

Fingertips

English Word Assistance: It provides pronunciation, meaning, and intelligent scoring of English words, offering timely feedback to help with practice.

Chinese Word Foundation: The lamp demonstrates standard stroke order for Chinese words, laying a solid foundation.

Content Dictation: Real-time feedback is provided based on the learning requirements of different school ages.

Online Study Room

Study with Buddies

Nightly online study rooms for different subjects enable students to study with peers of the same age and receive online tutoring from real teachers. Teachers also designate high-performing students as role models, effectively boosting learning motivation.

Self Study

Personalized Learning Plans: Students can create their own learning plans and use features such as word-searching and voice questioning on the lamp to guide their learning.

Homework Upload: Completed homework can be easily uploaded to parents for review and feedback.

Rewards and Medals: System rewards and "medals" are given to students upon plan completion, which can be used to access interesting stories or popular science content.

Dual Camera Video Call

Remote Parental Guidance: Through the mobile app, parents can remotely tutor and check in with their children.

Real-time Interaction: Parents can switch between the front and top cameras during video calls to proactively connect with their children, understand their learning difficulties, and provide guidance when needed.
